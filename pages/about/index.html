About

Hi, my name is Bruno Oliveira. This is a blog for me to talk about programming in general, share cool libraries I've discovered and projects I am working on.

I've been a programmer for over 18+ years and working professionally for over 14+ years, most of them at ESSS.

My day-to-day work involves mostly desktop application development in Python and C++, but my personal interests include DevOps, web applications, cloud computing and other development languages.

I'm one of pytest core maintainers and author of several plugins, including pytest-mock, pytest-qt and pytest-faulthandler.

You can follow this blog by signing for the RSS feed or by following me on GitHub.

Thanks for reading!
